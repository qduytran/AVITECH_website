• Researcher at AVITECH, VNU-UET

Tran Viet Khoa received his Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU-UET) in 2008. In 2010, he completed his Master of Science program in Information, Systems and Technology at Paris-Sud University, France, with a specialization in wireless networks and communication. He obtained his Ph.D. degree through a joint doctoral program between the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU-UET), and the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia (from November 2021 to May 2024). His research interests include cyberattack detection, cybersecurity, machine learning, deep learning, blockchain, and IoT security.
