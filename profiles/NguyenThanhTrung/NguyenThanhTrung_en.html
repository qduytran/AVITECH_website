• Lecturer at the Faculty of Information Technology, University of Information and Communication Technology, Thai Nguyen University, Vietnam.

Nguyen Thanh Trung received the Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronics and Telecommunications from the Faculty of Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi, in 2003. He obtained the Master of Science degree in Electronics Engineering from the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi, in 2012, and was awarded the Doctor of Philosophy degree in Electronics Engineering from the same institution in 2024. He is currently a lecturer at the Faculty of Information Technology, University of Information and Communication Technology, Thai Nguyen University, Vietnam. His research interests include biomedical signal and image processing, sparse representation and coding, and machine learning.
